# About Me

Richard E. White is a technical writer and author with a background in emergency medical services, organizational leadership, and professional writing. With over a decade of experience documenting high-stakes situations, he specializes in translating complex processes into clear, structured, and user-focused content.

He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Writing and English, graduating *summa cum laude*, and is currently pursuing a Master of Fine Arts in Creative Writing.

In addition to his technical work, Richard is a self-published author whose writing often explores themes of resilience, identity, and psychological depth. His growing focus on technical writing reflects his interest in systems, secure communication, and real-world applications of structured documentation, particularly within networking and technology environments.
